Karmin Corp

Karmine Corp (French pronunciation: [kaʁmin]; or simply KCorp) is a French professional esports organization incorporated in Tours and headquartered in Paris, France. The team employs professional players across six divisions, namely League of Legends,TrackMania,Teamfight Tactics, Super Smash Bros. Ultimate,Valorantand Rocket League.

Veno

Harry "Veno" Pearson is an English Professional Fortnite player. He began to make a name for himself when he finished second in the FNCS All-Star Showdown in Solo. Since then he has been one of the very best Fortnite players dominating everyone on the highground with different players such as aqua and Queasy, the last with whom he has dominated the competitive scene from July 2022.

Partners

LOGITECH

Logitech G is a subsidiary of Logitech International, the world leader in PC gaming equipment. The company's goal is to provide players with high-quality equipment so that they perform at the highest level. Thus, Logitech is a leading supplier and manufacturer in the market of keyboards, mice, headsets, mouse pads and is a pioneer in simulation equipment. With a sleek and innovative design, Logitech G products allow our gamers to compete at a high level.

SONIX

SONIX provides a communications solution for esports teams and other video game-related uses. The SONIX application stands out from its competitors for its much lower latency, which makes communication between players easier and faster, as the company claimed in a press release. The beta version of the SONIX application is currently under development, and the partnership with Karmine Corp will allow the team's expertise to be used to further guide the development of the final product. Players from Karmine Corp will contribute and help improve SONIX's offerings for esports players.
